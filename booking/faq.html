Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the hotel and the type of reservation you make. Generally, you can find the cancellation policy details on the hotel's page during the booking process or in your confirmation email.

How do I modify my booking?

To modify your booking, please log into your account, go to 'My Bookings,' find the booking you want to change, and follow the on-screen instructions. If you face any issues, you can contact our customer support for assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and some hotels may also offer alternative payment methods like PayPal. You can see the available payment options during the checkout process.
